About Me

Paul Anuoluwapo
Oladeinde.

I help leaders move faster by handling everything they should not have to.

Paul Anuoluwapo Oladeinde is a Lagos based Virtual Executive Assistant with over three years of experience supporting CEOs, MDs, and founders remotely. He brings a rare combination of operational precision and strategic thinking to every role he takes on.

Paul is known for being proactive before problems arise, detail oriented in everything he touches, and dependable when it matters most. He does not just manage tasks. He builds the systems, workflows, and structures that keep businesses running smoothly behind the scenes.

From calendar and inbox management to operations hubs, CRM systems, and workflow automations, Paul works at the intersection of executive support and operational excellence. His clients do not just get an assistant. They get someone who thinks like an operator.
